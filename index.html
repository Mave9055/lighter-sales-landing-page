Historical Significance & Verification

Provenance Decoding (Class 65-9WA):
  • 65: Fiscal Year 1965
  • 9: Class Number 9
  • W: Warrant Officer Candidate (WOC) Course
  • A: Section A

The Hughes TH-55A Osage helicopters were first delivered to the U.S. Army in December 1964. This timeline confirms that Class 65-9WA was indeed the very first group to train and graduate on this new airframe, replacing the older Hiller OH-23s. This lighter serves as a verified artifact of that aviation milestone.
